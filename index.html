About

Hi, my name is An-toni-na and I'm from Kyiv, Ukraine ♥♥. I've spent the last eight years in the US, splitting time between SF and NYC. I love both, but undeniably, I vibe better with the latter.

After moving to the US, I got into blockchain engineering and later found myself in DeFi — finally loving what I do and connecting the dots of my past: a math & physics lyceum, an applied math master's, and systems programming at an undisclosed R&D lab.

Currently, I'm leading the engineering team at M0, building in the hottest 🔥 and most stable corner of the industry, while slowly working toward a second degree in finance from Harvard Extension School. When TradFi & DeFi worlds merge into something productive, I want to be there too.

When I'm not working or chasing kids around (which is almost never), I like to get out and explore the countless beautiful places in and around NYC.
